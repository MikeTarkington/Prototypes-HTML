What is Digital Citizen?

A specialized social networking site that ties political info to fun, easy, and productive resources for empowering citizens to govern.

 

What does it offer?

• Connect with people active on political issues.

 

• Discover your personal political identity.

 

• Use calendars to create/find political events.

 

• Politician profiles enhance transparency, accountability, and communication.

 

• Create and participate in robust and productive moderated debates.

 

• Ongoing development to add a wide range of politically empowering features.
