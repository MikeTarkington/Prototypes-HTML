My Counterweight

 

The Counterwieght is a section designed to introduce us to the most popular content from people with opposing or different points of view.  Digital Citizen hopes to mitigate some of the discord resulting from deep seeded polarization.  If we interract with people who share sharply contrasting views we may come to realize that we share more core values than we have opposing ones.  The content here is displayed based on your responses to our Questionnaires which can be repeated/edited thus potentially altering the content displayed here.  More thorough completion of questionairres will produce more accurate results for content displayed here.

 

Keep an open mind, better understand people with opposing views, keep things civilized, and recognize that many political decisions are very complicated and come down to moral questions human beings have struggled with for all of recorded history.
