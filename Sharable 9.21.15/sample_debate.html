Does technology like the Digital Citizen site, and tools that government uses to better interract with the people it represents, hold the key to a better society?  I believe we can evolve the nature of our social interractions and in the process create greater political awareness and access to participation than the world has ever seen.  Goverment should invest in utilizing and developing cutting edge tech.
