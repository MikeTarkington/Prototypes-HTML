On digiciti.org you are rewarded for being an active user of site.  Badges are icons appearing on your profile celebrating your achievements and contributions to the Digital Citizen network.  Creating real world political change may seem impossible but our honor badges are symbols of recognition for being brave, intelligent, open-minded, creative, resourceful, and fun in our efforts to improve society through political means.

X

What are badges?

Why do they matter?

How can I get my hands on them?

Badges should be a source of pride for every user, and create an atmosphere of gamesmanship in which we strive to earn higher level badges and the social clout associated with mastery of specific badge categories.  Badges give us a fun and lighthearted reward for participating in some potentially difficult political activities.  More of the public will find political particpation bearable if there are some perks to logging in and becoming a digital citizen.  Those of us who already take an interest in politics are even more likely to make meaningfull online interractions if there is a fun perk as part of the process.

Each category of badge type appears on this page and if you hover over the heading you'll see a description of which digiciti.org activities will earn you points towards your next badge in that category.  There is usually a fairly intuitive relationship between the badge categories and the activities that earn points.  The points earned accumulate and hitting certain point thersholds will award you increasingly attractive badges to be unlocked and shown alongside your profile.  If you like your old badges you can show them on your profile instead of your higher level badge from that category.  If you don't like badges at all you can hide your badge earnings from other users.  Different amounts of points can be earned for each activity within a category, for example, if you created an active debate from scratch you would earn 1,000 points in the category but if you merely commented on someones debate you would only earn 250 points in the debate category.  The Profile badge category is unique in that points can be earned for it by meeting thresholds in other categories.
