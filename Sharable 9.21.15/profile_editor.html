The selections of ideology and party affiliation can be changed at any time and can be made private.

They help digiciti figure out who you're most likley to affiliate with, and who politically opposes you, so that the site can introduce you to people and content from people with varius political backgrounds.

Ideologies define broad political belief systems.

A main charactersitic of the conservative ideology means you are likely to want to keep things the way they are and maintain tradition.  In contrast, a liberal ideology often centers on promoting change.  These are loaded terms so it's important to consider their most basic principles and avoid missinterpretation.

Party affilliation such as Republican or Democrat is frequently associated with assessment of ideology.  Republicans tend to be more conservative and Democrats tend to be more liberal in their policy choices.
