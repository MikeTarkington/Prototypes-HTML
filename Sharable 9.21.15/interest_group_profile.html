TEG's overall mission is to use newfound powers of technology to enhance political engagement.  We accomplish this by working with leaders in political science, and tech developers to create innovative solutions for enhancing the accessibility and impact of citizen political engagement.  We also advocate for application of existing technology resources which can improve government function.  Efforts are coordinated through traditional and new technological means to exemplify how well the two methods of political engagement can work in conjunction.
